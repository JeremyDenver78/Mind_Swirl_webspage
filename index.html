About the App

[cite_start]

Mind Swirl is an iOS application designed to provide anxiety relief through a collection of calming, interactive mini-games[cite: 139]. Experience a soothing aesthetic with infinite gradients and soft UI elements designed to help you relax.

Mini-games include:

  • Jigsaw Puzzle: Piece together beautiful landscapes.
  • Magical Swirl: Create soothing trails of light with your touch.
  • Bubble Path: Gently guide a bubble through a floating path.
  • Sandfall: Draw patterns with falling sand.
  • Harmonic Bloom: Visualize sound in real-time.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Mind Swirl handles your data.

Microphone Usage

The "Harmonic Bloom" game requires access to your microphone to transform sound into visual art.

  • No Recording: We do not record, save, or store any audio data.
  • No Transmission: Audio data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is never sent to any server or third party.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect personal information, usage data, or tracking information.
