About the App

Mind Swirl is an iOS application designed to provide anxiety relief through a collection of calming, interactive mini-games. Experience a soothing aesthetic with infinite gradients and soft UI elements designed to help you relax.

Mini-games include:

  • Jigsaw Puzzle: Piece together beautiful landscapes.
  • Magical Swirl: Create soothing trails of light with your touch.
  • Bubble Path
